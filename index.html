About the recipes

My recipes follow a few principles:

  • Made with organic ingredients,
  • Healthy,
  • Outright delicious,
  • Easy to follow.
I believe everyone can cook. Saying "I am not good at cooking" or "I can't cook" simply means that you are too lazy to grab a knife or pan and prepare your own food.

Why cooking?

  1. Research has shown that buying pre-cooked food, rather than cooking with primary ingredients, increases the chance of literally every single disease you can think of.
  2. You can decide how much sugar and salt you add based on your taste. My recipes, for example, are naturally low in salt and sugar - it takes a bit of adjusting, but it's worth it!
  3. It's fun! Play some music while you are at it - it decreases stress and increases wellbeing.
