  1. Apple-Cake - Vegan

      2. Ingredients:

    • 300g flour
    • 60g coconut sugar
    • 1 flax egg (2 tbsp water, 1 tbsp ground flaxseed)
    • 50 ml coconut/olive oil
    • 3 apples
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 lemon - juice and rind
    • cinnamon - to taste
    • 100ml vegetable milk (approx)

      Process:

    1. In a bowl, add 2 tablespoons of water and 1 of ground flaxseed to create a flag egg. Let it rest for 5 minutes.
    2. Slice 2 apples and turn them into a pulp (with a food processor).
    3. Slice thinly the other apple.
    4. Once the 5 minutes have passed, add the apple sauce, vanilla extract, lemon juice, vegetable oil to the bowl.
    5. Whisk briefly.
    6. Now add the lemon grated rind, the cinnamon, the baking powder, the coconut sugar and the eggs.
    7. Combine until homogeneous.
    8. Lastly, add the apples slices and the milk (not necessarily 100ml - if shouldn't be too liquid!) and mix again.
    9. Pour in a 20cm cake pan and place in the oven at 180* for about 35 minutes. Test with a toothpick before turning off the oven.
    10. Enjoy!

  2. Pancakes- Vegan and gluten-free

      3. Ingredients:

      -
    • 100g buckwheat flour
    • 100g rice flour
    • 60g coconut sugar
    • 30ml coconut oil
    • 2 tsp vanilla extract
    • 75ml vegetable milk (approx)

      Process:

    1. In a bowl, add all the solid ingredients, followed by the liquid ingredients.
    2. Once happy with the consistency (they should be runny but not liquid, able to keep the shape in the pan) warm up a pan and melt some coconut oil.
    3. Pour 1 fat tablespoon of batter onto the hot pan, cover and let it cook until bubbles appear - after which turn them and let them cook on the other side as well.
    4. Once enough of them are ready, eat with a sprinkle of soy yogurt, some seasonal fruit and some maple / coconut syrup.
